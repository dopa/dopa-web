justin schier

justin schier, co-founder and chief creative officer, dopamine inc.

JUSTIN SCHIER is a hybrid designer and developer with over 15 years of direct experience in the Engagement space. He has been a business owner since 1999, the lead developer on large projects for companies such as JD Power and Associates, Bentley Motorcars, USC, and at least 100 others. Most recently he has focused on Livecube, an innovative web app for events that uniquely fuses game mechanics and social media. He lives in New York City and blogs at Ghost Transmission.
